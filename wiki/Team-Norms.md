# Team Norms

How the Helios agile team works together. Living document — propose changes anytime.

## Ceremonies

| Ceremony | When | Duration |
|----------|------|----------|
| Daily stand-up | Weekdays 09:30 | 15 min |
| Sprint planning | Sprint day 1 | 2 hrs |
| Backlog refinement | Mid-sprint | 1 hr |
| Sprint review | Last day | 1 hr |
| Retrospective | Last day | 1 hr |

## Working agreements

- **Scope freeze after day 2** — new requests go to the backlog, not the current sprint.
- **Definition of Ready** — a story is only pulled into a sprint if it has clear acceptance criteria and an estimate.
- **One topic per doc page** — keeps reviews small and search useful.
- **Every decision gets recorded** — in the relevant meeting-notes page.

## Documentation conventions

- Write in Markdown; start from a [template].
- File names: lowercase, hyphenated, ID-prefixed (`hel-142-...`).
- Link related items instead of copying content.
